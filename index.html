About Nova Eve

Nova Eve is a nurturing mini-society where individuals transitioning from chronic homelessness can rebuild, reconnect, and reimagine their future. We provide shelter, skills training, and community support.

Our Programs

  • Life Skills Training
  • Therapeutic Support & Counseling
  • Community Housing Solutions
  • Job Readiness & Mentorship
